# Marketing Funnel - Knowledge Base

## Digital Marketing Funnel for Real Estate

## Awareness Stage (Top of Funnel)
- Content marketing
- Social media presence
- SEO optimization
- Paid advertising
- PR and media coverage

## Interest Stage (Middle Funnel)
- Lead magnets
- Webinars and events
- Email marketing
- Social media engagement
- Educational content

## Consideration Stage (Bottom Funnel)
- Property showcases
- Virtual tours
- Consultation calls
- Testimonials
- Case studies

## Conversion Stage (Action)
- Sales presentations
- Negotiation process
- Contract signing
- Payment processing
- Onboarding

## Retention Stage (Post-Sale)
- Customer support
- Community building
- Referral programs
- Upsell opportunities
- Loyalty rewards

## KPIs and Metrics
- Traffic sources
- Conversion rates
- Cost per acquisition
- Customer lifetime value
- Return on investment
