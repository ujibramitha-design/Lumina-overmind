# Privacy Policy
**Version 1.0**  
**Effective Date: January 1, 2024**

## 1. Introduction
Lumina Technologies ("we", "the company", or "Lumina OS") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data in accordance with Indonesia's Personal Data Protection Law (UU PDP).

## 2. Data We Collect
We collect the following personal data:
- **Identity Data**: Name, email, phone number
- **Business Data**: Company name, location, contact information
- **Usage Data**: Activity logs, user preferences
- **Technical Data**: IP address, device type, browser

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing
We process your data based on:
- **Consent**: For marketing and personalization
- **Contract**: For service provision
- **Legitimate Interest**: For analytics and security

## 4. Your Rights
Under the PDP Law, you have the right to:
- **Right to Access**: Access your personal data
- **Right to Correction**: Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure**: Request data deletion
- **Right to Portability**: Receive data in machine-readable format
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw consent at any time

## 5. Data Retention
We retain data according to the following periods:
- Profile data: 7 years after account closure
- Leads data: 5 years
- Analytics data: 2 years
- Marketing consent: Until withdrawal

## 6. Data Security
We implement:
- Data encryption in transit and at rest
- Role-based access controls
- Regular security audits
- Incident response protocols

## 7. Data Breaches
In the event of a data breach, we will:
- Notify you within 3x24 hours
- Explain impact and mitigation steps
- Report to relevant authorities

## 8. International Data Transfer
Your data is stored on servers in Indonesia. International transfers only occur with equivalent protection.
